About The Program

The Graphic Design and Web Design Program is designed to prepare students for entry-level positions in their perspective fields. The program emphasizes basic skills, knowledge, and abilities typically encountered in the graphic design professions. Instruction includes theories and methodologies used to implement creative, technical, and aesthetic solutions into print advertising, packaging, web, digital video and new media applications.
